For this year's festival we are getting our hands dirty! We will rehearse community practices and structures that can keep all of us alive - as alternatives to the oppressive systems around us - while diving into the messy waters of relating across our differences without falling into fragmentation. Through transformative education, this knowledge will be shared in non-hierarchical and liberatory ways, geared towards enacting change in our worlds. We center self-organization, experimentation, and care, with the goal of rehearsing the alternatives we want to build in this temporary prefigurative space.

Building. Here we get our hands dirty to practice how to grow, feed, and provide for our communities as we are tasked with the undoing and hospicing of ‘modern civilisation’, and the pillars of oppression and exploitation it stands on. Have you ever wanted to learn how to forage safely, build using natural materials or wire your getaway car? Get your elbows greasy and get stuck in!

Maintaining. Our communities need maintenance! Together, we will rehearse care practices, accountable relationships and generative conflict for continued commitment to the struggle. We will explore tools for movement building that center care and collaboration, processing emotions collectively and figuring out the complexity of working and living together

Connecting. How can we connect to other houses, villages, the world at large? We explore global resistance and solidarity with those far and near. It’s about building concrete alliances: sharing strategies, supporting each other’s struggles, and finding ways to act together beyond our immediate surroundings and struggles. Liberation means leaving no one behind!

Delinking. Forging a community for collective survival does not mean retreating into priviliged eco-villages on the outskirts of the capitalist system. It means breaking dependency from extractivist, exploitative and neocolonial supply chains that is profiting on the backs of the global majority. How can the North de-center itself, relinquish imperial privilege, and become a genuine partner in building a multipolar, ecological civilisation? We will practice anti-opression and use power-critical tools to unlearn and deconstruct, while focusing on building a global, anti-colonial network for redistribution.

Safekeeping. How do we keep each other safe - away from notions of militarization and heightened surveillance under the name of safety? In many ways, we are already operating from some of the safest contexts in the world — yet those who are Othered in the current system, and challenge power, face increasing risks. We want to practice community care by ramping up our skills for climate emergency response, self defense, digital security and action trainings to keep safe while doing the nitty gritty work of activism.
