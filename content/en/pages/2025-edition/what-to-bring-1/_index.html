Essentials
Tent / camper van
Sleeping bag (warm one!)
Light clothes for day and warm clothes for night
Rain coat (summer in the German countryside can be volatile, so bring good rain protection as we will be spending time outside)
Comfy shoes or sandals (20 min walk to the place)
Towel, tooth-brush & paste and any other biodegradable cosmetics if needed (we will provide body wash)
Head torch
Water bottle
Ear plugs
Swimming suit (fancy a dip in the river?)
Any required medication

Not required, but very welcome
Hammock
Blanket (for coziness)
Yoga mat (morning yoga can be cold on bare grass)
Clothes for swapping
Music instruments for jam sessions
Poetry, songs or other to share at the open mic session
Materials for your workshop (or you let us know of any needs so we
try to provide it)
Books for exchange
T-Shirts or bags for textile painting sessions


*Remember that the
nearest shop is quite a
walk away so make
sure you have all you need
for the week including
your snacks and treats
(organic, chemical, the
kind that produces smoke
and the kind that makes
you feel fuzzy) ;)
