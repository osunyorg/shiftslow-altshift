  • Tent / camper van 

  • Sleeping bag (warm one!) 

  • Light clothes for day and warm clothes for night 

  • Rain coat (summer in the Austrian countryside can be volatile, so bring good rain protection as we will be spending time outside)

  • Comfy shoes or sandals (20 min walk to the place is uphill) 

  • Towel, tooth-brush/paste and any other biodegradable cosmetics if needed (we will provide body wash) 

  • Head torch 

  • Water bottle 

  • Swimming suit (fancy a dip in the river?)

  • Any required medication

  • Hammock 

  • Portable solar panel for charging 

  • Music instruments for jam sessions 

  • Poetry, songs or other to share at the open mic session 

  • Materials for your workshop (or let us know ahead of time and we will try to provide it) 

  • Books for exchange 

  • Yoga mat (morning yoga can be cold on bare grass) 

  • T-Shirts or bags for textile painting sessions
