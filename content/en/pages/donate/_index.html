All donations go directly into the festival creation

We appreciate every donation, as they go directly into making the festival happen! Our team works entirely on a volunteer basis, so your support helps cover essential expenses and keeps the festival groovy, experimental and sustainable. 

How does it work?
To donate, just transfer the wished amount to our bank account (details below). If you'd like to donate and also get a gift from us, send us an e-mail with the subject 'Donation-Gift', indicating the exact gift and your address. We ship within Europe and will get the gift to you within a span of 3 weeks from when you ordered.
