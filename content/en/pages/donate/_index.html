We met at Vlio's place in Rotterdam in December 2023 for a little arts and crafts residency for the crowdfunding rewards and thought.. why not offer our friends to get one of our wonderful little goodies also after the time of the crowdfunding? Each of the pieces is hand-crafted with much love by Elena, Lara and Vlio.

To donate, just transfer the wished for amount to our bank account (details below). If you'd like to donate and also get a gift from us, send us an e-mail with the subject 'Donation-Gift', indicating the exact gift and your address within the e-mail.

We ship within Europe and will get the gift to you within a span of 2 weeks from when you ordered.
